# Code of Conduct

NeuroTrace is an open source project for collaborative technical work. We want
the community to be direct, curious, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat other contributors with respect, especially during disagreement.
- Keep critique focused on ideas, code, behavior, and evidence.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for the impact of your words.
- Welcome contributors with different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Share enough context that others can participate productively.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized comments or unwelcome attention.
- Publishing private information without clear permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Bad-faith behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or impractical.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainers through the
repository's private reporting channel when available. If a private channel is
not available yet, open a minimal public issue requesting maintainer contact and
do not include sensitive personal details.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block accounts, or take other
reasonable steps to protect the project community.
